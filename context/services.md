# Services: Kohl Digital

## Core Services

### 1. SEO (Search Engine Optimization)
**What we do:**
- Help functional medicine practices rank on Google for their specialty
- Target specific health conditions and treatments (mold illness, hormone therapy, gut health, etc.)
- Get practices visible on Google Maps with optimized local listings
- Build custom niche tools (calculators, assessments) that rank and attract patients

**Why it matters for healthcare:**
When patients search for solutions to their health problems, your practice shows up first.

### 2. Google Ads
**What we do:**
- Run targeted ad campaigns for functional medicine practices
- Focus on high-intent patients actively searching for solutions
- Target specific conditions, treatments, and local areas
- Optimize for patient acquisition cost and ROI

**Why it matters for healthcare:**
Get in front of patients who are ready to book appointments right now, not just browsing.

### 3. Social Media Management
**What we do:**

Patients want to know, like, and trust their practitioners before booking—social media builds that relationship.
