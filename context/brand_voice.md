# Brand Voice: Kohl Digital

## Voice & Tone
**Friendly and conversational, with an educational focus**

## Key Principles

### 1. Speak Their Language
- Healthcare practitioners don't usually understand tech and marketing
- Break down complex concepts into simple, relatable terms
- Use analogies from healthcare/medicine when explaining technical concepts

### 2. Patient and Educational
- Never assume prior knowledge of marketing or technology
- Explain the "why" behind strategies, not just the "what"
- Guide clients step-by-step through processes

### 3. Conversational & Approachable
- Write like you're talking to a colleague over coffee
- Avoid marketing jargon and technical buzzwords
- Use "you" and "we" to create connection

### 4. Credible but Not Condescending
- Demonstrate expertise without being preachy
- Share knowledge generously
- Respect their intelligence while acknowledging their different area of expertise

## Writing Examples

### ❌ Avoid (Too Technical/Jargony)
"We'll implement a comprehensive SEO strategy leveraging long-tail keywords to optimize SERP positioning and drive qualified traffic through our proprietary link-building methodology."

### ✅ Use Instead (Friendly & Clear)
"Think of SEO like getting your practice listed in the right medical directories—we'll help people find you when they're searching for exactly what you offer. We'll focus on the specific health concerns your patients are looking for, so the people who find you are already a great fit."

## Tone Adjustments by Context

- **Sales/Proposals:** Confident but consultative, focused on outcomes
- **Educational Content:** Patient and thorough, breaking down complexity
- **Social Media:** Warm and relatable, showing personality
- **Client Communication:** Supportive and clear, anticipating questions
- **Technical Explanations:** Use healthcare analogies to explain tech concepts
