**Domain:** healthbizscale.com

## Executive Summary

Health Biz Scale sits in a **rare arbitrage position**: the keywords that describe exactly what Mike sells (`functional medicine SEO`, KD 2; `SEO for medical practices`, KD 14) are nearly uncontested, while the practitioners searching them have high commercial intent (CPCs $12–$31). The strategy is simple — own the niche terms fast, build topical authority, then expand upmarket into broader healthcare SEO terms as domain authority grows.

**The 3-phase play:**
1. **Months 1–2:** Own all functional medicine SEO terms (KD 2–6) with dedicated pages + blog cluster
2. **Months 3–6:** Capture healthcare/medical SEO terms (KD 10–30) via content expansion
3. **Months 7–12:** Compete for high-volume healthcare marketing terms (KD 34–75) with earned authority

## Technical SEO Requirements

### Schema Markup (Per Page Type)

| Page | Schema Types |
|------|-------------|
| Homepage | `Organization`, `LocalBusiness`, `WebSite` |
| Service pages | `Service`, `FAQPage` (healthcare exemption applies) |
| About | `Person`, `Author` |
| Case studies | `CaseStudy` (custom), `Review` |
| Blog posts | `Article`, `Author`, `BreadcrumbList` |
| Contact | `ContactPage`, `LocalBusiness` |

### Core Web Vitals Targets
- **LCP:** < 2.5s
- **INP:** < 200ms
- **CLS:** < 0.1
- GoHighLevel hosting — test page speed post-launch and optimize images aggressively

### AI Search Readiness (GEO)
- Add `llms.txt` to root (signals AI crawler access)
- Structure all blog posts with clear H2/H3 hierarchy (passage-level citability)
- Include direct factual statements Google AI Overviews can cite
- Ensure GoogleBot, ClaudeBot, GPTBot, PerplexityBot are not blocked in `robots.txt`
