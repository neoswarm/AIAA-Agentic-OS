Common Questions

Questions About Growing Your Practice

What makes the SCALE SEO Framework different from standard SEO?

Standard SEO is generic. The SCALE Framework is built specifically for functional medicine and naturopathic practices — covering the trust signals, schema types, content topics, and local strategies that move the needle in healthcare. It also incorporates AI Overview optimization, which most agencies haven't touched yet.

I've tried other marketing companies and got nothing. Why would this be different?

Most agencies apply generic healthcare marketing to functional medicine practices — it doesn't work because the patient journey, trust dynamics, and search intent are completely different. We exclusively serve functional medicine and naturopathic practitioners, and every system we build is designed around how your patients actually search, decide, and book.

Can I implement these systems myself or do I need to hire you?

Both. The free resources and playbook are designed for practitioners who want to implement themselves. For those who want it done-for-you, we handle everything from setup to optimization. Most clients choose the done-for-you path because their time is better spent on patient care.

How long before I start seeing results from SEO?

Google Maps ranking typically moves within 30 days. Organic SEO results compound — most clients see meaningful traffic increases within 60–90 days, with consistent growth through month 6. The niche terms we target (functional medicine SEO keywords) have very low competition, which means faster movement than typical healthcare SEO timelines.

Do you work with naturopathic and integrative medicine practices too?

Absolutely. The SMART and SCALE systems were designed to work across functional medicine, naturopathic medicine, and integrative health practices. The patient acquisition principles are the same — the keyword targeting and content strategy adapt to your specific specialty and local market.
