Advanced Functional Medicine is dedicated to "transforming patient health through personalized care and evidence-based practices." The clinic prioritizes identifying root causes rather than managing symptoms alone, taking a holistic approach to wellness.

The practice operates on the principle that "biological dysfunctions and imbalances cause disease and illness." Unlike traditional medicine's symptom-focused approach, they emphasize identifying and treating root causes of health problems through personalized care.

**Source:** [About Page](https://www.advfunctionalmedicine.com/about-us/), [Functional Medicine Services](https://www.advfunctionalmedicine.com/specialties/functional-medicine/)

- Holistic scope: Addresses physical, mental, emotional, nutritional, and digestive wellness simultaneously

- Integrative methods: Combines lifestyle modifications, environmental interventions, and supplementation alongside conventional medicine
- Multidisciplinary team: Doctors, nurses, nutritionists, dietitians, health coaches, and holistic therapists work collaboratively
