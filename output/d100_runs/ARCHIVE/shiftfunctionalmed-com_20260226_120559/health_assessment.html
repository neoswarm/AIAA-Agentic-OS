Our Care Models

SHIFT's 7 Pillars of Health Optimization

Nutrition

Individualized plan to heal and optimize your gut health. Advanced testing for food sensitivities, microbiome dysbiosis, or increased intestinal permeability.

Relationship

Living in a healthy relationship both with yourself and your community is paramount to longevity. Practices such as mindfulness, meditation, prayer, or community involvement.

Healthy Environment

Identify and remove toxins in your home and environment. When indicated, test for toxins and guide you through a detoxification protocol.

Individualization

Differing genetic expression in all individualized treatment plans. Advanced genetic testing when indicated to optimize healing.

Rejuvenation

Rest and rejuvenation are integral to health. Optimal sleep and adequate self-care approaches to combat stress and feel energized.

Hormone Health

Healthy and balanced hormones are a key to vitality. Uncover hormonal imbalances and partner with you to optimize hormone levels.

Physical Activity Optimization

Elevate your game with a fitting and realistic plan to uplevel your health and meet your goals.
