Book an Appointment Services Offered Christie Brooks AFMC, MS, Functional Medicine RD About Me Christie Brooks is a Functional Medicine Practitioner. Additionally, she has been a Registered Dietitian (RD) for over 25 years and has 15 years of experience as a Diabetes Educator. Christie is a member of both the Institute of Functional Medicine and the School of Applied Functional Medicine. She completed her Dietetic Internship through Indiana University-Purdue University Medical School and obtained her Masters in Clinical Nutrition at the University of Arkansas for Medical Sciences. Christie and her husband have two young adult children. Christie’s history includes being a certified personal trainer, a Crossfit Level 1 trainer, a half marathon runner, a restaurant owner, Arkansas’ Emerging Dietitian of the Year (2006), coach of her daughter’s sports teams, an adjunct professor at Harding University, voted Best of the Best in the Arkansas Democrat Gazette 2021, a phlebotomist, an author, and a speaker. She has overcome a 9-year journey of an eating disorder that started at the age of 12. Her thirst for knowledge and understanding of the mind-body-spirit connection has become a passion, evident in her efforts to educate and empower others. Using patient-centered, evidence-based modalities for lifestyle medicine, Christie addresses the root causes of body imbalances, offering personalized wellness plans. Clients start with a “Symptoms to Solutions” consultation. Some need simple gut health repair for hormone balance and weight loss, while others have complex issues like adrenal or thyroid insufficiency, along with gut health and inflammation. Christie collaborates with all healthcare providers, all for the benefit of the client’s health journey. Christie’s passion is to help others improve their health through lifestyle medicine, which emphasizes movement and good nutrition, drawing heavily from the Creator’s pharmacy of colorful foods, herbs, and botanicals rich in phytonutrients, minerals, and vitamins. She assists people in healing to reduce reliance on prescription medications. You can find her book, “Root Cause: Turning Symptoms into Solutions,” on Amazon.
