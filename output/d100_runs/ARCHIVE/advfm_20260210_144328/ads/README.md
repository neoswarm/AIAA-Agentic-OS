## 🛠️ TROUBLESHOOTING QUICK REFERENCE

| Issue | Most Likely Cause | Quick Fix |
|-------|------------------|-----------|
| Low impressions | Budget/bids too low | Increase budget 30%, bids 20% |
| CTR <3% | Ad copy not compelling | Test headlines with numbers (96%) |
| CPC >$8 | Too competitive keywords | Pause expensive keywords, focus on long-tail |
| Low conversions | Landing page issues | Reduce form fields, add click-to-call |

## 🎓 CAMPAIGN PHILOSOPHY: DREAM 100 FRAMEWORK

These campaigns are built on the **Dream 100 principle**: target your ideal 100 customers with precision rather than broadcasting to everyone.

**How it's applied here:**
1. **High-intent keywords only** - We target people actively searching for solutions (not education)
2. **Specific pain points** - Ads speak to diabetes reversal, hormone issues, thyroid problems (not generic health)
3. **Clear differentiation** - Every ad emphasizes unique advantages (96% success, 200+ biomarkers, largest in CA)
4. **Transparent pricing** - Reduces tire-kickers, attracts serious buyers
5. **Friction-reducing CTA** - Free discovery call lowers barrier to entry

**Result:** Fewer, higher-quality leads at lower cost per acquisition.
