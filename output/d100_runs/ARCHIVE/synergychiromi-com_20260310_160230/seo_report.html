📊 Metrics that don't connect to revenue.

Traffic reports. Impression counts. Keyword rankings. These are the numbers agencies can control — and the only ones they talk about. What you actually need is a straight line from spend to booked patients. Most agencies can't draw that line because they've never been held to it.

What we do instead: Every campaign we run traces directly to patient inquiries. If we can't show the line from ad spend to revenue, we don't run it.

💸 Ad spend without intent targeting.

Broad keywords fill pipelines. They just fill them with the wrong people. A patient searching "fatigue doctor near me" is infinitely more valuable than someone searching "health clinic." Most ad managers don't make that distinction — or don't know how to build campaigns around it.

What we do instead: We only bid on searches from patients who have already decided they need help and are looking for someone specific. Zero broad match. Zero wasted impressions.

🔍 SEO built for search engines, not patients.

Generic content farms write about your conditions using medical jargon that reads well to algorithms and terribly to patients. You rank for something no one searches. Or you rank for something people search but don't convert — because the page doesn't speak their language.

What we do instead: Every page we write is built around how your patients actually describe their symptoms, their fears, and the outcome they want. That's what ranks and converts.

🤖 AI tools built for someone else's business.

Most AI tools were built for Amazon sellers, real estate agents, and generic SaaS companies. When healthcare practices try them, they spend months customizing something that was never designed for clinical workflows, HIPAA-sensitive communication, or the complexity of a multi-provider practice.

What we do instead: We don't pick a tool and hand you a login. We build from scratch, around exactly how your practice runs. Your team uses it from day one.
