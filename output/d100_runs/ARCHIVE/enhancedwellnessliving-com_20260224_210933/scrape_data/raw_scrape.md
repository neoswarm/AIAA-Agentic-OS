If your weight isn&#8217;t where you want it to be, you think about changes that you can make in order to send the number on the scale in the right direction. However, if you really want your weight loss journey to be effective, you need to do more than focus on your total weight. You also need to look at body composition and take a new approach to shaping your body to fit your needs. Understanding Body Composition Body composition looks at the big picture. It is comparing the proportion of the amount of fat in your body as opposed to everything else that is fat-free. In other words, it is taking into account your bones, organs, muscles, and the water content in your body. When you look at it in percentages, your percentage of fat should be smaller when compared to your percentage of bones, organs, and muscles. When you prepare yourself for a weight loss journey, taking a measurement of your body composition can help you to reach your end goal. Know exactly what you are dealing with in the beginning, understanding that your fat-free mass is a plus. Your muscles, bones, and tissues are going to work for you, burning off calories. Fat will not. Understand that your age, your hormone levels, your genetic history, and whether you are a man or a woman will affect your body composition. How to Use Your Body Composition Measurements to Your Advantage Once you have an assessment of your body composition, you can set goals that are specific. Focus on how much fat you need to take off.
