Meet Dr. Lynese Lawson, MD

Dr. Lynese L. Lawson, MD

Founder & Medical Director — Proactive Wellness Centers, PLC

Dr. Lawson founded Proactive Wellness Centers on the premise that most health problems can be prevented or reversed when identified early and treated at the root cause. Based in Vienna, VA, she specializes in functional and preventive medicine — with deep expertise in MTHFR gene mutation, medical weight loss, testosterone replacement therapy, heart attack prevention, and comprehensive cardiometabolic screening. Dr. Lawson orders the tests most primary care physicians never consider, and interprets them in the full context of your hormones, metabolism, genetics, and cardiovascular risk simultaneously.
