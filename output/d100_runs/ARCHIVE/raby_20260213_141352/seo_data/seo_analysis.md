# SEO Analysis: Raby Institute

## What's Actually Happening With Your Website

Your organic traffic is at 6.1K visitors per month with 1,400 keywords ranking. You have a solid Authority Score of 27 and 357 referring domains pointing to your site. Traffic share from organic is 54%, which means more than half your visitors are finding you through Google.

## Bottom Line  

You're getting good search visibility for a local integrative medicine practice in Chicago. The 1,400 keywords show Google sees you as relevant for many health-related searches. But there's room to grow—most of these rankings could move higher with focused effort.

## What the Data Shows

- **6,100 monthly visitors** from Google search
- **1,400 different keywords** you show up for
- **Authority Score 27** (good for local healthcare)
- **2,700 backlinks** from other websites
- **+0.9% keyword growth** over the past year

Your top referring sites include healthgrades.com and usnews.com, which are high-quality medical directories that boost your credibility with Google.

## What Changed And Why It Matters

Your organic traffic dropped -19% recently. This could be from Google algorithm updates, competitors improving their SEO, or seasonal factors in healthcare searches. The keyword count grew slightly (+0.9%), so you're not losing ground dramatically, but momentum has slowed.

## The Real Problem

You have 1,400 keywords ranking, but many are on page 2-5 of Google. Getting to the top 3 positions dramatically increases clicks. A keyword ranking #12 gets almost no clicks. The same keyword at #3 gets hundreds.

## Why This Is Actually an Opportunity

Your foundation is strong. You already rank for the right topics. Now it's about moving those rankings from page 2 to page 1, and from position 5 to position 1. Small improvements in rankings = big increases in traffic.

## If Nothing Changes

You'll keep getting 6K visitors per month. Your competitors who invest in SEO will slowly take your rankings. Healthcare searches are competitive in Chicago—practices that don't actively improve their SEO gradually lose visibility.

## If This Is Handled Correctly

Focus on your best opportunities:
- Move keywords ranking #8-#15 to top 5 (quickest wins)
- Create content targeting "integrative medicine Chicago" variations
- Build more backlinks from Chicago health directories
- Optimize for "acupuncture Chicago," "functional medicine Chicago," "naturopathic doctor Chicago"

A 50% traffic increase (9K visitors/month) is realistic within 6 months with consistent SEO work.
