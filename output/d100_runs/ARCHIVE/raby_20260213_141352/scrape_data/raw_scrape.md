## CLINICAL APPROACH

### Philosophy
The Raby Institute takes a comprehensive, patient-centered approach to healthcare, combining the best of conventional medicine with evidence-based complementary therapies. We believe in treating the whole person—body, mind, and spirit—rather than just symptoms.

### Differentiators
1. **Team-Based Care:** Multi-disciplinary team of MD, naturopaths, acupuncturists, and manual therapists
2. **Root Cause Focus:** Extensive diagnostic testing to identify underlying causes, not just symptom management
3. **Evidence-Based Integration:** Combining proven conventional and complementary therapies
4. **Personalized Protocols:** Individualized treatment plans based on comprehensive assessment
5. **Large, Established Practice:** Well-resourced center with diverse specialty expertise
6. **Northwestern Affiliation:** Academic medical center connection ensuring high standards

### Treatment Modalities
- Functional medicine testing (comprehensive labs, microbiome, hormones, genetics)
- Clinical nutrition and therapeutic diets
- Botanical medicine and nutraceuticals
- Acupuncture and Traditional Chinese Medicine
- Manual therapies (naprapathy, soft tissue work)
- Mind-body medicine
- Bioidentical hormone replacement when appropriate
- Lifestyle medicine (sleep, stress, movement, relationships)
- Integrative cancer support
